Product Description

Experience premium sound quality with the SoundMaster SM-450X wireless headphones. These over-ear headphones feature advanced active noise cancellation technology that blocks out ambient noise, allowing you to fully immerse yourself in your music, movies, or calls.

The ergonomic design with plush ear cushions ensures all-day comfort, while the 30-hour battery life means you can enjoy uninterrupted listening for extended periods. With Bluetooth 5.0 connectivity, you'll experience stable wireless performance with a range of up to 30 feet.

Key features include:

  • Advanced Active Noise Cancellation
  • 30-hour battery life with quick charge (5 min charge = 2 hours playback)
  • Built-in microphone for hands-free calls
  • Touch controls for easy playback and volume adjustment
  • Foldable design with included carrying case
